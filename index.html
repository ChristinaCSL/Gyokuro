GYOKURO GREENTEA

Our green tea is a Japanese green tea from the Uji City. It is infused with jasmine blossoms making it delicate and refreshing. Our green tea offers whole leaf sachets with no dust or fannings. 100% biodegradable sachets; 20 count. Recommended steep temp: 180-185 degrees.

OUR STORY

Our story begins in 1950 in the Fukujuen greentea capital of the world — Uji, JP. It was here that founder and owner Hashira Yamato followed his passion and started Fonté Tea Factory. Our name is Japanese for source. We help tea lovers by brewing the freshest cup of tea.

GYOKURO SERVICES

We believe in partnerships. Your business deserves personalized services for your entire coffee experience from installation and training to assistance when the unexpected happens.
